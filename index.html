Oregon Water Polo Foundation


Oregon Water Polo Foundation is committed to excellence in and outside the pool. We believe in the development and preparation of each of our athletes, both physically and academically. We believe that in order for our athletes to succeed at the collegiate level, they need to have exposure and coaching from those who have been there. We believe in building the character of every athlete so that they will be successful wherever life may take them. Last but not least, we believe in changing the culture of Oregon water polo
